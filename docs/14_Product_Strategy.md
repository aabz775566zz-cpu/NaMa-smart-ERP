# ERP Smart Product Strategy

# استراتيجية المنتج


# 1. Product Vision

## رؤية المنتج


ERP Smart aims to become an AI-powered business operating system for small and medium businesses.


الهدف:

إنشاء نظام يساعد صاحب العمل على إدارة وفهم شركته بسهولة.


---

# 2. Initial Target Customers

## العملاء المستهدفون في البداية


We will not target everyone.


Initial customers:


## Small Retail Businesses

Examples:

- Shops.
- Small distributors.
- Local businesses.


Why:

They need:

- Inventory.
- Sales.
- Customer management.
- Simple reports.



---

# 3. Customer Problems

## مشاكل العملاء


Main problems:


- Using spreadsheets.
- Losing sales information.
- Poor inventory tracking.
- No clear business insights.
- Expensive ERP systems.



---

# 4. Product Positioning

## مكانة المنتج


ERP Smart is:


"Simple enough for small businesses, powerful enough to grow with them."


---

# 5. Business Model

## نموذج الربح


SaaS Subscription Model


Users pay monthly or yearly.



---

# 6. Subscription Plans


# Free Plan


For testing.


Includes:

- Basic account.
- Limited products.
- Basic customers.



---

# Basic Plan


For small businesses.


Includes:

- Full inventory.
- Sales.
- Customers.
- Reports.



---

# Professional Plan


For growing companies.


Includes:

- More users.
- Advanced reports.
- AI assistant.
- Automation.



---

# 7. Pricing Philosophy


Price should be:

- Affordable.
- Easy to understand.
- Worth the value provided.



---

# 8. Launch Strategy


Phase 1:

Build MVP.


Phase 2:

Release to first users.


Phase 3:

Collect feedback.


Phase 4:

Improve and expand.



---

# 9. Customer Feedback Loop


Always collect:

- Feature requests.
- Problems.
- User behavior.
- Suggestions.



---

# 10. Success Metrics


Measure:


Product:

- Active users.
- Companies registered.
- Retention rate.


Business:

- Paid customers.
- Monthly revenue.
- Customer satisfaction.



---

# 11. Product Principles


Always build:

- Simple solutions.
- Real customer value.
- Features users need.


Avoid:

- Feature overload.
- Unnecessary complexity.
